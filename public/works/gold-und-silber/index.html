gold und silber

abstract

In February 2018 i started a three-month internship as a gold- and silversmith at Parolari Goldschmied AG Frauenfeld

i learned (on a basic level) to cut, saw, file, drill, solder, bend, mill, pull and cold-forge in silver, brass and copper.
